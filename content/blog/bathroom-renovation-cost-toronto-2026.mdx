## How Much Does a Bathroom Renovation Cost in Toronto?

Planning a bathroom renovation in Toronto? You're about to make one of the smartest investments in your home. But first—you need accurate numbers.

The honest reality: **Toronto bathroom renovation costs range from $8,000 to $60,000+** depending on scope, size, and finish level. Here's what you actually need to know.

## 2026 Toronto Bathroom Renovation Price Ranges

### Basic Refresh: $8,000 – $15,000

A basic bathroom refresh keeps the existing layout intact and focuses on cosmetic upgrades:
- New vanity and faucet
- Toilet replacement
- Tile resurfacing or basic new tile
- New lighting
- Paint and accessories

**Best for:** Guest bathrooms, rental properties, budget-conscious updates.

### Mid-Range Full Renovation: $15,000 – $28,000

This is the most common bathroom renovation in Toronto. It includes:
- Full gut and rebuild (new drywall, subfloor)
- Custom tile work (floor and walls)
- New plumbing fixtures (shower, tub, toilet, vanity)
- Frameless glass shower or tub surround
- Pot lights and exhaust fan
- New vanity with storage

**Best for:** Main bathrooms, primary ensuites, homes preparing for sale.

### Luxury Ensuite: $28,000 – $60,000+

A luxury ensuite renovation transforms your bathroom into a spa-like retreat:
- Designer fixtures and hardware
- Heated floor systems
- Steam shower enclosures
- Custom cabinetry and double vanity
- Smart lighting and controls
- Premium large-format tile

**Best for:** Master ensuites, luxury condos, long-term family homes.

## Key Factors That Affect Your Bathroom Renovation Cost

1. **Bathroom size** – A 50 sqft powder room costs far less than a 120 sqft ensuite
2. **Plumbing changes** – Relocating the toilet, shower, or bathtub adds $2,000–$8,000
3. **Tile selection** – Imported tile vs. domestic tile can swing costs by $3,000–$10,000
4. **Permit requirements** – Structural changes, plumbing relocation, or new additions require permits
5. **Accessibility features** – Walk-in showers, grab bars, and wider doorways add cost but improve safety
6. **Waterproofing quality** – Always budget for proper moisture barrier—it prevents future damage

## FAQs: Toronto Bathroom Renovation Costs

**How long does a bathroom renovation take in Toronto?**
A mid-range renovation typically takes 2–4 weeks. Complex renovations with plumbing relocation or structural changes may take 5–8 weeks.

**Do I need a permit for a bathroom renovation in Toronto?**
Cosmetic updates (fixtures, tile, vanity) typically don't require permits. Moving plumbing or structural walls requires a City of Toronto building permit.

**What adds the most value in a bathroom renovation?**
Frameless glass showers, heated floors, quality tile, and proper ventilation deliver strong ROI in Toronto's real estate market.
