Finishing your basement in Pickering or Ajax adds 500-1,000 sq ft of living space at **$60-100 per square foot**—far cheaper than adding an addition or moving to a larger home.

## Waterproofing Requirements (Critical for Durham Region)

Pickering and Ajax homes often have high water tables and clay soil. **Budget $8,000-15,000 for proper waterproofing** before finishing.

### Interior Waterproofing
- **Sump pump system**: $2,500 - $4,000
- **Interior weeping tile**: $5,000 - $8,000
- **Vapor barrier upgrade**: $1,200 - $2,000

### Exterior Waterproofing
- **Excavation & membrane**: $10,000 - $18,000 per side
- **Full perimeter**: $25,000 - $45,000

**When needed**: Active leaks, foundation cracks, musty smell

> **Ajax/Pickering tip**: Get a waterproofing inspection BEFORE starting your reno. Finishing over wet basements = mold disaster.

## Durham Region Permit Requirements

Both Ajax and Pickering require building permits for basement renovations.

### What Requires a Permit
✅ Adding/removing walls
✅ Electrical work (new circuits)
✅ Plumbing (bathroom/kitchen)
✅ Creating a secondary suite
✅ Installing egress windows

### Permit Costs
- **Pickering Building Department**: $350 - $600
- **Ajax Building Department**: $300 - $550
- **Processing time**: 3-5 weeks

### Required Inspections
1. Framing/insulation
2. Electrical rough-in
3. Plumbing rough-in
4. Final inspection

**Cost**: Included in permit fee

## Egress Window Requirements

For legal basement bedrooms or apartments, Durham Region requires:

- **Minimum opening**: 3.8 sq ft (0.35 m²)
- **Minimum height**: 15 inches (380mm)
- **Minimum width**: 20 inches (510mm)
- **Maximum sill height**: 44 inches above floor

**Cost per window**: $3,500 - $5,500 (including excavation, window, well)

## Common Pickering/Ajax Basement Issues

### 1. Low Ceilings
Many 1960s-70s homes have 6'6"-6'8" ceilings. Minimum code is 6'5" for living space.

**Solutions**: 
- Dig down (expensive: $50,000-80,000)
- Accept lower resale appeal
- Use only for rec room (not apartment)

### 2. Foundation Cracks
Ajax/Pickering clay soil causes settlement cracks.

**Fix before finishing**: $800-2,500 per crack (Epoxy/polyurethane injection)

### 3. Old Oil Tanks
Some older Ajax homes have buried oil tanks.

**Removal cost**: $3,000-6,000 (required by law before major renovations)
